Vedic Maths

Introduction

Vedic Mathematics is a system of mathematical techniques that originated in ancient India. It involves a set of short-cuts and tricks to solve mathematical problems quickly and efficiently.

History

The origins of Vedic Maths can be traced back to ancient Indian scriptures known as the Vedas. These mathematical principles were rediscovered and popularized by Sri Bharati Krishna Tirthaji Maharaja in the early 20th century.
